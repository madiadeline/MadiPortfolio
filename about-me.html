"I don't mind when I muck up my work, because I know that there is always a way to improvise and make it better.."

Growing up, I loved to doodle I still do. I will doodle over everything! Whether it be phone books while i'm making a call, paper work while I'm filling out forms, I even doodled on my mirror when I was young, moustaches, crazy eyebrows and all that jazz. It was a habbit. But, I really enjoyed it. So I embraced it. I've aways been a sketcher but I enjoy working with charcoal, paint and other mixed media just as much.
